Essential Skincare Tips

Sun Protection

Always wear sunscreen with at least SPF 30, even on cloudy days. Reapply every 2 hours when outdoors.

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water and use a good moisturizer to keep your skin hydrated throughout the day.

Beauty Sleep

Get 7-9 hours of sleep daily. Your skin repairs and regenerates itself while you sleep.

Gentle Cleansing

Use a gentle cleanser twice daily. Avoid hot water as it can strip natural oils.

Daily Skincare Routine

Morning Routine

  1. Cleanse face gently
  2. Apply toner
  3. Use serum
  4. Apply moisturizer
  5. Finish with sunscreen

Evening Routine

  1. Remove makeup thoroughly
  2. Double cleanse
  3. Apply treatment products
  4. Use night cream
  5. Apply eye cream

Tips by Skin Type

Dry Skin

  • Use cream-based cleansers
  • Apply rich moisturizers
  • Avoid hot water
  • Use facial oils

Oily Skin

  • Use gel-based products
  • Don't skip moisturizer
  • Try clay masks
  • Use oil-free sunscreen

Combination Skin

  • Zone-specific treatment
  • Balance hydration
  • Use mild products
  • Regular exfoliation

Sensitive Skin

  • Patch test new products
  • Use fragrance-free items
  • Minimal ingredients
  • Gentle application
